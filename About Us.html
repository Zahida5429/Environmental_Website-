About Us

We are a company specializing in Remediation,Restoration, Remodeling and Organic Home Cleaning. Through our work, we discovered a significant link between toxins in household air and the illnesses people were experiencing. We realized that, by raising awareness and helping people maintain healthy homes, much of this sickness and suffering could be prevented. For over a decade, we've collaborated with scientists, consulted holistic health experts, and leveraged our own experience to uncover the best methods for creating healthy air at home, all in support of a healthier lifestyle for everyone. JM Environmental Services embodies our vision of a company dedicated to fostering better living through cleaner air. Whether your focus is on wellness, recovery, or home restoration, we are committed to supporting you every step of the way on your journey towards a better, healthier life.

OUR MISSION

To end the air quality epidemic and alleviate human suffering by harnessing the power of healthy indoor environments that foster wellbeing.

OUR VISION​

We envision a world where a paradigm shift in indoor air quality leads to healthier individuals and happier families.

CORE VALUES

Everyone at JM Environmental practices C.A.R.E.

Clarity – The clarity at our core between our staff and clients enables us to provide a clear path to improving your health.

Accountability – Each day, we take responsibility for the conduct, performance, decisions, and actions of our personnel, products, and services.

Responsibility – Each employee not only cares deeply but also takes responsibility for your home as if it were their own. We don’t just aim for a job well done; we commit to scientifically removing the indoor pathogens harming your family.

Elevation – We continuously evolve, adapt, and enhance our technology to empower our entire team to better serve, educate, and assist our clients.
